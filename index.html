Katherine Gorham

Katherine Gorham is a New York based writer, comedian, and baby dance teacher. Her silly and wholesome fictional podcast, Frog College, premieres September 3rd, and she is currently working on scripts for a pilot and a feature film.

Her writing is character-driven and very funny. It has been described by her family as “actually a real podcast” and “pretty good!” She hopes to write something like The Office, New Girl, and Madmen, and to represent realistic female characters with specific skills and goals.

Katherine has a BA in Theater from Stony Brook University and continues her education at the Upright Citizens Brigade in New York City. Her sketch group, Really Great Dads, produces sketches and performs improv at various locations.
